Lead with Value — What RA Actually Does
🔒
Privacy
Bizee's address appears on public state records — not the customer's home address. Keeps them off data broker databases that scrape state filings.
🛡️
Protection
Bizee receives legal documents and state correspondence, scans them, and uploads to the dashboard. A missed legal notice can result in a default judgment.
✅
Ease
Being your own RA requires being physically available at a street address every business day, all year. Bizee handles this with staffed offices in all 50 states.
Call Flow — Pricing Pushback
1

Listen first

Use active listening to clearly understand the customer's concern before offering any resolution. Thank them for being a Bizee customer. Recognize their concern. Don't rush past it.

2

Acknowledge, then pivot

Open with one sentence: "We've updated our pricing to reflect better what's covered under your registered agent service." Then pivot to value. Don't apologize. Don't list reasons. Don't say you held prices low for years.

3

Lead with value, not price

Use the moment to walk through what RA actually covers — privacy, protection, and ease. Let the value speak before any numbers come up.

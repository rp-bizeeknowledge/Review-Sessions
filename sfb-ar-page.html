What Changed

Customers placing an Annual Report order are now offered the option to add the State Filings Bundle (SFB) subscription, waiving the processing fee.

The Annual Report processing fee increased from $99 to $129 as of April 27, 2026.

Cancellation & Refund Process

When a customer with an SFB subscription requests cancellation of their Annual Report, a pop-up will appear in the system. You must confirm which option the customer wants before proceeding.

A
Full Cancellation

Cancel / Refund

Cancels both the AR filing + SFB subscription, and issues a full refund.

  • Before presenting this option, confirm customer eligibility by checking both cancellation policies.
    • AR C&R Guidelines SFB C&R Guidelines
  • Customers may have placed additional state filing orders using the waived processing fee this makes them ineligible for an SFB refund.
B
Keep Subscription

C/R & Keep Subscription

Keeps the SFB subscription active and cancels only the AR filing. Refunds the State Fee exclusively.

  • Confirm eligibility by reviewing the Annual Report cancellation policy
    • AR C&R Guidelines
⚠ Only the State Fee is refunded under this option. The SFB subscription cost is not refunded and remains active.
Sales Updates
1

When helping a customer choose between options, set clear expectations upfront about pricing, renewal rate, and what is included in each option.

2

Lead with value, not price. Use the interaction as an opportunity to highlight the State Filings Bundle unlimited filing fees waived for all supported filings within the year.

3

Always check the Resource Guide to confirm product availability before quoting what is covered by the SFB.
